CONTROLS:

W: forward

A: turn left

S: reverse

D: turn right



E: open door

Q: close door


T: Switch to point light

R: Switch to directional light



L: Look at car

K: Look at origin



UP ARROW: Move camera toward origin

DOWN ARROW: Move camera away from origin

LEFT ARROW: Rotate camera clockwise about origin

RIGHT ARROW: Rotate camera anti-clockwise about origin
